What We Built
Three Layers of Value from a Single Stack

At the foundation: a registered search engine with years of investment linking global RSS feeds across national press, trade press, regulatory bodies, government releases, specialist vertical publications, corporate disclosures, and non-English sources. This ingestion infrastructure is difficult to replicate because it is labour-intensive, requires continuous maintenance, and requires editorial judgement in feed selection. The value does not begin with AI. It begins with structured data capture at global scale.

Layer two: a narrative intelligence engine that doesn't just count keywords or score sentiment. It understands pacing, escalation, sentiment drift, threat clusters, and structural pressure. Traditional news reports what happened. Noah extracts what is emerging.

Layer three: calibrated prediction and probability reporting — the ability to produce reports with projections that would have taken six months of industry surveying, updated daily on any vertical.

The Breakthrough
Narrative Intelligence That Predicts Markets
"Our reports are so accurate that we can predict Polymarket movements six hours ahead of the way people are thinking."

This is not sentiment analysis. Noah's Bionic Ear reconstructs the narrative fabric of any subject — tracking how stories propagate, mutate, accelerate, and collide across millions of sources in real time.

The Adriana Report applies Bass Diffusion Modelling to narrative data, mathematically separating innovation-driven coverage (PR, advertising) from imitation-driven spread (organic, viral). This tells you whether a story is a firework or a wildfire — and projects time-to-peak before a single data point appears in traditional metrics.

The Strategic Crossroads
Category Leadership, Not Revenue Harvesting

Noah sits at a rare inflection point. It can either continue selling SaaS-like intelligence tools into slow, struggling legacy publishers — or become a vertically structured intelligence publisher and infrastructure layer.

The first path generates revenue. The second path builds category leadership.

The definition of "consumer" has shifted. Modern B2C includes hedge fund managers, active retail traders, defence analysts, policy advisors, corporate strategists, risk underwriters, and communications directors. These are individual subscribers or small teams willing to pay for edge.

Acquisition opportunity: If capital were available, Noah could acquire distressed mastheads, retain their brand equity, replace analogue workflows with the intelligence engine, launch verticalised subscription products, drastically reduce production cost, increase content volume, and improve analytical quality.

Legacy publishers are shrinking. AI-native intelligence publishers do not yet exist at scale. Helping rescue analogue businesses is long-cycle, political, and low-multiple. Replacing them is high-risk, high-multiple, and category-defining.
